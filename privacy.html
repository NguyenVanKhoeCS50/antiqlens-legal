AntiqLens — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 16, 2026

AntiqLens ("the app", "we") is an AI-powered antique identifier. This policy explains what data the app handles, where it goes, and why. By using AntiqLens you agree to this policy.

1. Accounts and how we identify you

You can start using AntiqLens without creating an account — a private, anonymous guest profile is created for you automatically so your scans and collection can be saved. You may later sign in (for example with Google) to back up and sync your collection across devices; when you do, your existing guest data is linked to that account. We store an account identifier and, if you sign in, the email address provided by your sign-in provider.

2. Photos you scan

When you scan an item, the photo is sent over an encrypted connection to our server and forwarded to a third-party AI provider (such as OpenAI and/or Anthropic) to generate the identification and value estimate. Regarding these scan images:

  • The full-resolution image used purely for AI analysis is processed in memory and is not stored on our servers.
  • If you save an item to your collection, a compressed display image and thumbnail are stored in our cloud storage (Firebase) so the item appears in your collection across your devices.
  • Scan content is not used to train AI models and is not sold.

3. Collection and scan data

Items you save — identification results, value estimates, your notes, albums, and history — are stored in our cloud database (Google Firebase) and associated with your account so they sync across your devices. You can delete individual items, or request deletion of your account and all associated data (see Contact below).

4. AI value estimates — important

Identifications, condition assessments, and value figures are AI-generated estimates, not professional appraisals. They may be inaccurate. Do not rely on them for buying, selling, insurance, or financial decisions without independent expert verification.

5. Purchases

Subscriptions are processed entirely by Google Play (Android) or the App Store (iOS). We never see or store your payment details. Your subscription status is verified through the store and recorded against your account so your Pro features are available across your devices.

6. Permissions

  • Camera — used to photograph items for scanning. Required for the core feature.
  • Photos (optional) — if you choose to scan an existing photo instead of using the camera.
  • Notifications (optional) — used for price alerts and updates; the app works without it.

7. Service providers

We use Google Firebase (authentication, database, storage, cloud functions, and messaging) to run the app, and third-party AI providers (such as OpenAI and Anthropic) to analyze scans. These providers process data on our behalf under their own security and privacy commitments.

8. Analytics & tracking

The app does not include third-party advertising SDKs. We may use privacy-respecting, aggregated diagnostics to keep the service reliable.

9. Data retention & your rights

Your collection data is retained while your account is active. You can delete saved items at any time in the app, or contact us to delete your account and all associated data. Depending on your region (e.g. GDPR / CCPA) you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your data; email us to exercise them.

10. Children

AntiqLens is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from them.

11. Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new "Last updated" date.
